Rosary School Camp celebrates Navratri by organising garba contest

On September 30, 2022, the Rosary School Camp, celebrated Navratri festival by conducting a garba competition in the school. Students from Nursery, Junior KG, Senior KG and primary sections participated in this competition. All students wore colourful outfits and danced to the beat of authentic garba music fully enjoying every moment. Prizes were given for ‘best dress’, and ‘dance’. It was a difficult task to select the winners as everyone was smartly dressed. The event was enjoyed by one and all and the students and staff tried their best to make this event, a grand success.
